# AgentStack.io - Affiliate Program Guide

This guide contains the official links and simple steps to register for the high-paying SaaS affiliate programs needed for AgentStack.io.

## 1. Make (Integromat) - Workflow Automation
- **Sign up link:** https://www.make.com/en/affiliate
- **Commission:** 20% recurring commission on all paid plans.
- **How to get your link:**
  1. Go to the sign-up link above and click "Apply Now".
  2. Fill out the application form (mention you have a directory of AI integration tools).
  3. Once approved (usually fast), log in to your dashboard and copy your unique referral link (looks like `make.com/register?pc=YOUR_ID`).

## 2. Jasper - AI Marketing Copilot
- **Sign up link:** https://www.jasper.ai/partners
- **Commission:** 30% recurring commission for life.
- **How to get your link:**
  1. Click "Apply to be a Partner" on the page.
  2. Complete the PartnerStack application.
  3. After approval, log into PartnerStack, go to Links, and copy your default affiliate link.

## 3. Retool - Internal Tools Builder
- **Sign up link:** https://retool.com/partners/affiliates
- **Commission:** 30% commission for 12 months for every new paid customer.
- **How to get your link:**
  1. Apply through their Rewardful integration page.
  2. Create an account and verify your email.
  3. Your unique link (e.g., `retool.com/?via=YOUR_NAME`) will be displayed immediately on the dashboard.

## 4. Chatbase - Custom ChatGPT for Websites
- **Sign up link:** https://www.chatbase.co/affiliates
- **Commission:** 20% recurring commission.
- **How to get your link:**
  1. Click "Become an Affiliate". They use Rewardful.
  2. Sign up with your email.
  3. Copy your link from the dashboard instantly.

## 5. Zapier - The Automation Standard
- **Sign up link:** https://zapier.com/experts/affiliate-program
- **Commission:** Varies (often one-time bounty per sign-up or percentage based on campaign).
- **How to get your link:**
  1. Apply through the Impact Radius network (link provided on their page).
  2. Await approval (might take a few days).
  3. Generate your tracking link inside Impact Radius.
